ConnectStory

US-ASEAN Connect was launched in February 2016 as the U.S. Government’s new, unifying framework to deepen the United States’ growing economic engagement with ASEAN and the ten ASEAN Member States. Connect seeks to deepen cooperation within the U.S. Government, provide opportunities for robust private sector engagement, and strengthen the economic ties between the United States and ASEAN. ASEAN Member States collectively are the United States’ fourth largest trading partner, supporting jobs in the region and the United States.
